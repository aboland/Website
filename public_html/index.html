Aidan Boland

I am a Postdoctoral researcher in the Insight Center for Data Analytics at UCD with Andrew Parnell . My current project is in conjunction with Clavis-Insight and focuses on machine learning algorithms, in particular supervised and unsupervised classification.

Publications

Noisy Monte Carlo: convergence of Markov chains with approximate transition kernels (2015). Statistics and Computing.

Background

I obtained my PhD under the supervisor on Nial Friel at UCD (2015). My PhD research focused on overcoming intractable likelihoods in Bayesian analysis. I investigated using Markov Chain Monte Carlo to target tractable approximate posterior distributions which were ‘close’ to the true posterior distributions for Gibbs random fields.
